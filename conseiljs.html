ConseilJS

ConseilJS is a multi-chain client-side library written in TypeScript, currently focused on Tezos, and available through NPM. ConseilJS makes it easy to effortlessly read from and write to blockchains. Along with Conseil, it forms a spine for building permissioned and decentralized blockchain apps.

A technical overview and documentation for ConseilJS can be found on the ConseilJS wiki.
