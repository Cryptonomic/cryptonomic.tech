Support

Galleon Wallet

Galleon is distributed through the Microsft Store for Windows and Snapcraft for Linux. Signed releases are available for macOS on GitHub. Galleon Mobile is available for iOS devices.

Paid prior support with a two business day SLA is available for users across the world. If needed, a member of our team can jump on a Hangouts or Skype screenshare with you. Please email support@cryptonomic.tech to use the service. Note that we do not offer wallet password or key recovery services.

For free community-based support, visit our ZenDesk support portal or join our public chat rooms on Matrix / Element.
