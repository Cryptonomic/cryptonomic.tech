Rhubarb

Rhubarb is an upcoming Web application designed to mitigate the risks of digital asset transfers through asynchronous sends.

Funds are sent to a smart contract where they are securely parked until the recipient claims them or the sender reverses the transfer. This product helps prevent user errors such as fat fingering, sending assets to the wrong accounts, ensuring a safer and less stressful transfer process.
