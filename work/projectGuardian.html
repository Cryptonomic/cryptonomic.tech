Project Guardian

Cryptonomic participated in the first phase of Project Guardian, an ambitious effort under the aegis of the Monetary Authority of Singapore. In this pilot, JP Morgan, the Development Bank of Singapore and SBI (Japan) participated in a lending platform with tokenized real world assets to demonstrate the applicability of DeFi to institutional finance.

We played a key part in the overall product and system design and built all primary components, including the smart contracts.
