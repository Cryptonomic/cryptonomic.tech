Persimmon

Persimmon is a mobile application built for those who prioritize privacy, security and decentralizing by self-hosting their crypto keys.

When self-hosting, it’s difficult to regularly track all digital wallet activities. This increases the risk of events like unnoticed instances of theft, missing staking income and missed DeFi activity.

In order to help those who self-host, Persimmon allows you to set up trackers and receive daily alerts about any balance changes.
